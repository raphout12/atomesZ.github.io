Game scenario

In the year 2100, a mysterious meteorite hits the Earth. A team of researchers from different nationalities were allowed to conduct research on this meteorite. After several years of research, they discovered an overpowered source of energy from the fragments of this meteorite, which unfolds around the world. Meanwhile, similar meteorites hit the earth from time to time, expanding technology and advancing research. However, as meteorite crystals become scarce, tensions develop between nations until 2142, during which time searches are made for the creation of elemental ammunition weapons, a meteorite of above average size collapses on earth, creating a new world war. The game then takes place in the year 2184, during which the fight is more violent than ever, and where each meteorite crash gives way to violent conflicts. Our two teams then consist of federal agents and gangsters, wishing to recover fragments of meteorites scattered around the crash site.
